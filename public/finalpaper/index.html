Psych 711 Learning from language

Syllabus Schedule Final Paper

Final Paper

You will write your final papers in groups of ~3. Each group has a choice to write a (1) Grant proposal (2) Review paper on a topic within the scope of this class (3) An empirical paper using online data collection, novel analyses of new data, corpus analysis or simulation.

Workshopping papers

We will have a writing workshop on 12/1.

Final Presentations

Each group will present their papers on the last day of class (~17 min presentation + 10 min Q&A)
